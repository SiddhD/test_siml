Roasting of sphalerite in the presence of lime and steam

Objective

To roast the given sphalerite concentrate in the presence of lime with an addition of steam and to analyze the calcine.


Apparatus used:

Furnace, filter paper, Ammonium Chloride, Ammonium Hydroxide, Beaker, Stirrer, Lime, Sphalerite, Steam, Porcelain boat.


Description Roasting : It is a process of heating sulphide ores in presence of air at high temperatures to convert them into oxides.

Using lime and steam in roasting : This helps in reducing the amount of sulphur di oxide emissions in traditional roasting methods.
STEP  1 

 Measure the weight of the empty petridish

STEP  1 

 Transfer the contents to a porcelain boat

STEP  2 

Place the porcelain boat in the even zone of the furnace. Add steam.


STEP  3 

About 1g of Calcine is weighed accurately and dissolved in hot water. ZnSO4 is passed into the solution.
